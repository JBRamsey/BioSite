Christopher Boldt is Systems Administrator for a Staffing Company in the Green Bay area of Wisconsin. Living in Wisconsin allows him the chance to enjoy all four seasons, of which his favorite is winter for its ability to completely transform a landscape. Christopher is an accomplished and passionate academic, with degrees in network administration and history. He changed careers in 2018 after being laid off from his previous job, after which he went back for his second degree in network administration (and has a desire to continue his education into a third). He has a love of history, which he continues to express in his taste in movies and games and has a little-known side-business as an independent author, where he publishes under a pseudonym.

His greatest role-model is Napolean Bonaparte, of which Christopher has read his collected personal writings. He enjoys Napoleon’s writings on Caeser and learning about how someone who did so much to change the world lived in his day to day (for instance, his ‘mind cabinet’, which involved visualizing putting away each topic of thought at the end of the day).

In keeping with his love of history, Christopher loves to travel, and has visited Washington DC and Philadelphia, and intends to go to Yellowstone later this year. After that, he has plans to see London, Rome and Tokyo alongside his friends. Christopher’s hobbies include gaming, biking and watching movies. He watched over 100 movies last year alone, an activity he also enjoys doing with his friends.
