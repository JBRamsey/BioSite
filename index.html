Meet an individual with a deep passion for history, academia and technology. Boasting two college-level degrees in history and network administration and a near third in political science, Systems Administrator Christopher Boldt is a lifelong academic and scholar. As a systems administrator for a staffing company based in Wisconsin, Christopher revels in independent projects and finds joy in tackling diverse challenges. He models his life after his life-long role model, Napolean Bonaparte, from whom he draws inspiration. Having overcome a significant career change in 2018, Christopher now sets his sights on a future of extensive travel with his closest friends.
